# Privacy Policy — GIFT Quiz Editor Plus

**Last updated: April 21, 2026**

---

## Overview

GIFT Quiz Editor Plus is a Google Forms Editor Add-on that allows users to create quiz questions using the GIFT text format. This policy explains what data the add-on accesses, how it is used, and what is never collected.

---

## Data We Access

| Data | Why |
|---|---|
| The active Google Form (questions, structure) | To create, replace, or append questions on your behalf |
| GIFT text you type into the sidebar | To parse and convert it into Google Forms questions |

---

## Data Storage

- **Document Properties (Google's servers):** The last GIFT text you submitted is saved to the form's document properties via Google's `PropertiesService`. This data is stored by Google, tied to the specific form, and is only used to restore your last session when you reopen the sidebar in that form.
- **Browser localStorage:** The sidebar autosaves your in-progress GIFT text to your browser's `localStorage`. This data never leaves your device.

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect any personal information.
- We do **not** transmit any data to external servers.
- We do **not** use analytics, tracking, or advertising.
- We do **not** read any Google Drive files other than the active form.
- We do **not** store data beyond what is described above.

---

## Third-Party Services

This add-on does not integrate with any third-party services. All processing happens locally in your browser or within Google's own Apps Script infrastructure.

---

## Permissions

The add-on requests the following OAuth scopes:

| Scope | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/forms` | Read and modify the active Google Form |
| `https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.container.ui` | Display the sidebar UI inside Google Forms |

These are the minimum permissions required for the add-on to function.
