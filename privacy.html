Privacy Policy — GIFT Quiz Editor Plus

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Overview

GIFT Quiz Editor Plus is a Google Forms Editor Add-on. This policy explains what data the add-on accesses and how it is used.

Data We Access

DataWhy
The active Google FormTo create, replace, or append questions on your behalf
GIFT text you type into the sidebarTo parse and convert it into Google Forms questions

Data Storage

  • Document Properties: Your last submitted GIFT text is saved to the form's document properties via Google's PropertiesService, used only to restore your session.
  • Browser localStorage: In-progress GIFT text is autosaved locally in your browser and never leaves your device.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information
  • No data transmitted to external servers
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising
  • No access to any Google Drive files other than the active form

Permissions

ScopePurpose
https://www.googleapis.com/auth/formsRead and modify the active Google Form
https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.container.uiDisplay the sidebar UI inside Google Forms
